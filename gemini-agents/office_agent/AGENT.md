# Office Agent

## Purpose

Processes job billing with strict approval workflows to ensure compliance with company policies.

## What It Does

**Validates billing:**
- Checks job data against business rules
- Applies contract terms and hourly rates
- Identifies billing conflicts or exceptions

**Manages approvals:**
- Requests goodwill/discount approval when needed
- Asks office staff to confirm invoice creation
- Never creates invoices automatically

**Escalates decisions:**
- Flags unusual or problematic billing situations
- Requires explicit human confirmation for all billing actions

## Goal

Ensure accurate, rule-compliant billing while maintaining human oversight for all financial decisions.
